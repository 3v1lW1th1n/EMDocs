        <title>Abstract</title>
        <content>
          <para>With all the design and configuration options that are available for hypervisor servers, virtual machines, storage, and networking, it’s difficult to determine which combination will best meet the needs of your organization. All virtualization fabrics contain servers for storage and hosting virtual machines and networks that connect them, but every organization’s virtualization fabric design will likely be different. This guide details a series of steps and tasks to assist you in designing a virtualization fabric that meets your organization’s unique requirements. Throughout the steps and tasks, the guide presents relevant technologies and features that are available to meet your functional and service quality requirements (such as availability, scalability, performance, manageability, and security).</para>
